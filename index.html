— Section I The Service

Three pillars of quiet stewardship.

— 01

On-site cataloging

A specialist arrives privately, equipped to assess each piece — precious metal content, mintage, grade, provenance, and condition — with the discretion of a private banker.

— 02

Archival documentation

Every item is photographed, measured, weighed, and indexed against authoritative references. The result is a museum-grade dossier prepared for insurance, estate, and posterity.

— 03

Digital vault access

Your collection is delivered into the Collectibles Vault application — a private, encrypted digital ledger of holdings, valuations, and provenance, accessible only to you.

— Section III The Process

Measured, methodical, private.

— STEP 01

Consultation

A confidential conversation establishes scope, sensitivities, and the contours of your collection.

— STEP 02

On-site Visit

Our specialist conducts the physical inventory at your residence, vault, or safe-deposit facility.

— STEP 03

Documentation

Each item is catalogued, photographed and recorded in a private dossier prepared for your records.

— STEP 04

Vault Activation

Your archive is provisioned into the Collectibles Vault app and handed over with personal training.
